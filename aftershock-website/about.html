Our Mission

Currently, in the NHL, professional spotters in the crowd determine if a player needs to be tested for concussions. This can be controversial because it is based on human perception. The objective is to build an under-helmet device to reduce the number of undetected concussion-level impacts in hockey. The device provides coaches and trainers with data that allows them to make data-driven decisions. Coaches and trainers have access to all of their player's historical and real-time data. The device will constantly be accumulating data points that can be used to facilitate concussion research.
