Weekly Blog #4

Feb 18 - March 2

The team members are continuing to work on their individual components of the project and how the components will connect together to form the completed product. This is a continuation of the previous update blog so reading that before continuing is highly recommended.

Software

The ability to add players from the app has been added and is working smoothly. Additionally, push notifications are up and running.

Via exploratory testing (completed by team members assisting Eric), some bugs in the user interface (broken buttons, player icon sizing etc...) were identified last week. Eric has worked hard to resolve these issues and has the UI looking sleek.

Electrical

The design of the wire harness has been finalized. This includes wire lengths, wire routing, and wire labelling. To account for the elastic fabric being used in the design, a method of securing the wires throughout the skullcap with built in slack has been designed and tested on scrap fabric.

Mechanical

The new segmented 3D printed enclosure is finished printing. The new design allows for easier assembly and better facilitates wires routing from the outside of the box to the Arduino Nano. The components fit well inside. Integrating the box to into the skullcap is the next step, as well as working on heat shrinking the sensors.

Project Management

Despite some unexpected purchases we are still within budget. The timeline is tight however we believe we are still on track.

Marcus Laferriere

Mechanical Lead
